Pele: Birth of a Legend

Here's a time line of Pele's life:

  • october 21 1940 Birth of Edson Arantes do Nascimento (Pele)

    Father was a professional soccer player who would train him into becoming the best soccer player in the world

  • 1956 - 1974 played for Santos

    1088 goals in 1115 appearances

  • 1958 FIFA world cup

    youngest player in tournament and youngest to ever play in world cup. Scored first goal in the quarterfinals against Wales. Finished the tournament with 6 goals

  • 1962 FIFA world cup

    Pele was ranked best player in the world at the time and was expected to lead Brazil to victory. He got injured in a game but Brazil was able to carry on and win their 2nd World cup

  • 1975 - 1977 played for New York Cosmos

    64 goals in 104 appearances

"Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, love of what you are doing or learning to do"

-- Pele
